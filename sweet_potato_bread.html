Sweet Potato Bread

A nutritious and tasty treat for your furry friend!

Search: None / Category: All / Without: None / Use: Any

Ingredients

(Makes about 3-4 small pieces, suitable for small dogs)

  • Boiled or steamed sweet potato – 1 medium (about 100g) → Peel and mash it well.
  • Egg – 1
  • Rice flour or oat flour – 3 tbsp
  • Water or unsweetened milk – 2 tbsp (for adjusting the dough consistency)
  • Coconut oil or olive oil – 1 tsp

TIP!

  • You can add finely chopped chicken, carrot, or salmon for extra nutrition!
  • A tiny bit of honey can be added, but avoid making it too sweet.

Oven Version

  • Mash the Sweet Potato: Peel and mash the boiled/steamed sweet potato until smooth.
  • Make the Batter: Mix the mashed sweet potato with one egg. Add the rice flour (or oat flour) and mix well. Gradually add water or unsweetened milk to adjust the dough consistency. Finally, add coconut oil (or olive oil) to make the dough moist.
  • Shape the Dough: Roll the dough into small balls and gently flatten them. Alternatively, use a muffin tray for a more uniform shape.
  • Bake: Preheat the oven to 170°C (340°F) and bake for 15-20 minutes. The bread should be slightly golden on the outside but soft inside.

Microwave Version

  • Mash the Sweet Potato: Peel and mash the boiled/steamed sweet potato until smooth.
  • Make the Batter: Mix the mashed sweet potato with one egg. Add the rice flour (or oat flour) and mix well. Gradually add water or unsweetened milk to adjust the dough consistency. Finally, add coconut oil (or olive oil) to make the dough moist.
  • Shape the Dough: Roll the dough into small balls and gently flatten them. Alternatively, use a muffin tray for a more uniform shape.
  • Prepare for Microwaving: Pour the batter into a microwave-safe cup, silicone mold, or mug. Avoid making the batter too thick to ensure even cooking.
  • Cook in the Microwave: Heat at 600W for 2-3 minutes. Check in between and add 30 seconds if needed. Be careful not to overcook, as it can become too dry!
